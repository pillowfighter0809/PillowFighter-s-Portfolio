## Key Takeaways

* AI search optimization is not just traditional SEO renamed.
* Content quality, source credibility, and citation-worthiness are becoming more important.
* AI can increase content production speed, but distribution and brand visibility remain major challenges.
* Brands need to track how they are represented in AI-generated answers, not only how they rank in Google.
