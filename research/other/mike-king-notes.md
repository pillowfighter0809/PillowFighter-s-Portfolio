## Key notes
- SEO is shifting from ranking-focused optimization to AI visibility and relevance.
- AI search requires brands to think about citations, inclusion, and machine-mediated relevance.
- Content needs to be structured in a way that AI systems can understand and retrieve.
- Traditional traffic metrics may become less reliable as AI search increases zero-click behavior.

## Possible playbook insights
- Optimize content for retrieval, citations, and inclusion in AI-generated answers.
- Build structured, clear, authoritative content that AI systems can understand.
- Measure visibility beyond clicks and rankings.
- Treat AI search as both a discovery and brand visibility channel.
