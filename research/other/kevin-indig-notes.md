## Key notes
- AI and automation can help SEO teams mine insights from forums, SERPs, and ranking content.
- AI can support draft creation, but human understanding of the audience remains important.
- SEO strategy should combine qualitative and quantitative insights.
- AI search changes how marketers think about traffic, visibility, and content performance.

## Possible playbook insights
- Use AI to speed up research and first drafts.
- Mine customer pain points from forums, search results, and community discussions.
- Combine data analysis with audience research before creating SEO content.
- Treat rankings and traffic as signals, but also consider brand visibility in AI search.
