## Key notes
- Content creation alone is not enough; distribution is a major part of growth.
- AI can increase content production, but distribution determines whether content reaches the right audience.
- B2B SaaS content should be repurposed across multiple channels.
- Strong content systems combine creation, distribution, measurement, and iteration.

## Possible playbook insights
- Do not only use AI to create content faster; use it to support distribution planning.
- Repurpose one strong content asset into multiple formats.
- Build repeatable content distribution workflows.
- Measure content success beyond traffic, including engagement, visibility, and business outcomes.
