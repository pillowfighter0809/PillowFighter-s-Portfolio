## Key notes
- SEO can support product-led growth when content connects directly to product use cases.
- Demo-led SEO can help prospects understand product value before speaking to sales.
- Organic content should be tied to signups, pipeline, and revenue.
- B2B SaaS buyers often want to experience the product before committing to a sales call.

## Possible playbook insights
- Create content around product use cases and demo experiences.
- Use SEO to reduce friction in the buyer journey.
- Measure SEO success through signups and revenue impact, not only traffic.
- Combine AI-assisted content production with product-led and demo-led growth strategies.
