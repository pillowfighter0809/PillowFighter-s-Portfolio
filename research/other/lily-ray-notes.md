## Key notes
- AI search makes brand visibility more complex than traditional rankings.
- SEO teams need to understand how AI systems mention, cite, and describe brands.
- Google Search Console does not always give enough detail for AI Overview or AI Mode performance.
- Topical authority and relevance may become more important in AI-generated search results.

## Possible playbook insights
- Track brand visibility across AI search tools, not only Google rankings.
- Build content around strong topical authority.
- Monitor how AI systems describe the brand and competitors.
- Use third-party tools or manual checks to understand AI search presence.
