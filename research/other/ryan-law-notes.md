## Key notes
- AI content should be judged by usefulness and value, not only by whether it was AI-generated.
- SEO content still needs strong editorial judgment.
- AI can accelerate content production, but quality control remains important.
- The main risk is not AI itself, but publishing generic content that does not help the reader.

## Possible playbook insights
- Use AI for drafts, research support, and content structure.
- Keep a human review layer for accuracy, originality, and usefulness.
- Measure content quality by reader value, not production method.
