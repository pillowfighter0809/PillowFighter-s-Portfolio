**Expert:** Kevin Indig

**Link:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NCbgNMbpDCY  
**Date collected:** 2026-07-01  
**Type:** YouTube video / webinar  

### Why this video was selected
This video is relevant because Kevin Indig discusses how AI Overviews are changing SEO, organic traffic, rankings, and content strategy.

### Summary notes
- AI Overviews are changing how users interact with search results.
- SEO teams need to understand how AI-generated answers affect visibility.
- Organic traffic and rankings may not be enough as standalone metrics.
- Content strategy should adapt to AI-driven search behavior.

- Use AI to support research and content workflows, but keep strategic review human-led.

**Link:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-4cu882OJ8E  
**Date collected:** 2026-07-01  
**Type:** YouTube video / SEO discussion  

### Why this video was selected
This video is relevant because it directly compares traditional SEO with AI search optimization, including GEO and AEO concepts.

### Summary notes
- Traditional SEO and AI search optimization overlap, but they are not exactly the same.
- AI search increases the importance of clear answers, citations, topical depth, and authority.
- SEO teams need to think beyond ranking pages and consider how AI systems extract and present information.
- Content should be structured to support both human readers and machine interpretation.

### Possible playbook insights
- Create content with clear sections, direct answers, and strong explanations.
- Optimize for both search engines and AI-generated answers.
- Track brand visibility across AI search experiences.

## Video 3: Ross Simmonds — Content Distribution in the Age of AI

**Expert:** Ross Simmonds  
**Video title:** Content Distribution in the Age of AI  
**Link:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VXxFJAg7YJw  
**Date collected:** 2026-07-01  
**Type:** YouTube video / marketing talk  

### Why this video was selected
This video is relevant because Ross Simmonds focuses on how AI affects content creation and distribution, which is important for scaling SEO content effectively.

### Summary notes
- AI can make content creation faster, but distribution remains a major growth challenge.
- Publishing content is not enough if there is no strategy for getting it in front of the right audience.
- Content teams should repurpose strong ideas across multiple formats and channels.
- Distribution should be treated as part of the content workflow, not an afterthought.

### Possible playbook insights
- Use AI to support distribution planning, not only drafting.
- Repurpose SEO content into LinkedIn posts, newsletters, sales assets, and short-form content.
- Measure engagement and business outcomes, not only content volume.
