# Research Methodology

## Topic
AI-powered SEO content production

## Research Goal
The goal of this research project is to collect high-signal insights from real practitioners in SEO, AI search, content marketing, and B2B SaaS growth. The collected material is intended to support a future playbook on how B2B SaaS companies can use AI to improve SEO content production.

## Expert Selection Criteria
Experts were selected based on:

- Practical experience in SEO, content marketing, or B2B SaaS growth
- Publicly available content such as articles, interviews, newsletters, podcasts, or expert profiles
- Relevance to AI search, AI-assisted content production, organic growth, or content distribution
- Clear connection between content strategy and business outcomes

## Collection Process
For each expert, I collected 1-2 strong sources such as:

- Expert profiles
- Articles
- Interviews
- Podcasts
- Research pages
- Agency resources
- Public content hubs

Each source was reviewed and summarized into a separate markdown file under `/research/other/`.

## Tools Used
- GitHub for repository organization and version control
- Cursor IDE for editing and organizing the research files
- Claude Code and Codex extensions for AI-assisted structuring, summarization, and documentation support

## Organization Structure
- `/research/sources.md` contains the full expert list, source links, annotations, and file references
- `/research/other/` contains expert-specific research notes
- `/research/linkedin-posts/` is prepared for LinkedIn post collections
- `/research/youtube-transcripts/` is prepared for YouTube transcript collections

## Current Limitations
Most of the initial collected material comes from public articles, expert pages, interviews, and content hubs. LinkedIn posts and YouTube transcripts can be added in later iterations if deeper platform-specific analysis is needed.

## Next Possible Step
The next step would be to turn these expert notes into a practical playbook covering:

- AI-assisted SEO research
- Content planning
- Drafting and editing workflows
- AI search visibility
- Content distribution
- Measurement and performance tracking
